My career path into the design world started with me reading a book about the basics of HTML one afternoon from the library and this was all that I needed to launch my web design career, and I haven't stopped since.

For the past 3 years I have been involved in 100+ web projects ranging from small to medium sized businesses, creating everything from logos, websites, user interfaces, brochures, business cards, video & photo editing, flash add banners and email newsletters.

I'm naturally a tech geek and loves anything to do with computers and devices. When I'm not pushing pixels and learning all there is about the web you can find me traveling around Asia, learning Japanese and dabbling in photography.

Fun Facts: Constantly trying make it better / loves exploring / obsessed with details & typography (not enough padding drives me crazy) / Inspired by all forms of design / hates IE / keen eye for simplicity / big advocate for web standards / has a crush on responsive design / middle name is Robert
