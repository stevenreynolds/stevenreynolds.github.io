A Little About Me

My name is Steven Reynolds and I love crafting the web.

I am a Freelance Web and Graphic Designer with a passion for clean, user-friendly websites who absolutly loves his job. Helping clients stay up-to-date on the newest web technology and design trends to create websites with beauty.

For the past 3 years I have been involved in 100+ projects ranging from small to medium sized businesses, creating everything from logos, websites, UI, brochures, business cards, video & photo editing, flash add banners and email newsletters.
